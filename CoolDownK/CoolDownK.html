Help

Welcome to the Cool Down Kingston, the app that helps you stay hydrated and cool during extreme heat. Use Location Search to find and zoom to your location (addresses, landmarks & intersections will all work). You can select a specific amenity or change the search radius to make your search more specific. Click on the red results button to see a list of location near you, click on listed location addresses to zoom to the specific location. Click on icons for more info.

KFL&A Public Health and the City of Kingston

The data within this application is provided and maintained by KFL&A Public Health and the City of Kingston. The application draws data from the Tap Into Kingston Program as well as the Cool Down Here program from KFL&A Public Health. The public Washroom data and the Splash Pad & Wading Pool data was provided by the City of Kingston

This application was developed by Kingston's GIS department with generous support from the Open City team. Contact the GIS department to provide technical feedback or to get access to the source code.

Content Partners

Make this application better by becoming a content partner! Owners of local fitness facilities or concerned citizens can register to become a Kingston Recreation Content Partner. Adding data is as simple as editing an excel file, check out the tutorial for more info on how to add data to this application. If you already registered as a content partner, you can access and edit the application database here.
